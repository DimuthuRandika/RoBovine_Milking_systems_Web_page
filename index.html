About Us

The Success History of RoBovine

Background

For over 8,000 years, humans have milked cows. While dairy processing has improved, milking remains labor-intensive and inefficient, hindering progress in the overall procedure.

Our Mission

RoBovine seeks to improve the welfare of dairy farm owners, workers, and animals by making the milking process more productive, efficient, hygienic, and environmentally friendly.

Our Product

RoBovine has been designing and field-testing for the last two years and now has a production-ready design. The RoBovine system will revolutionize the milking process by increasing farm productivity, lowering milking costs, improving cow comfort and stress levels, and reducing environmental impact.

What are our Products?

Our Products

A lightweight, ergonomic delivery system that automates the cleaning, disinfecting, stimulation, drying, and post-dip treatment of dairy cow teats. Consistent handling of the cow teat along with the ability to customize water temperature and air pressure reduces cow stress and increases milk flow-rate. Eliminates the need for human contact with the cow teat and reduces the incidence of bacterial infection.

Lowers chemical residue in the milk by avoiding over-spray of disinfectant onto the udder and by more thoroughly washing and drying the teat. Reduces the use of chemical solution by two-thirds and eliminates the need for towels.
